Overarching Theme

For our 4.5 virtual Classroom Meeting, we were asked to present our thematic statements to connect the three essays together. After receiving feedback, I settled on this: "Providing a new perspective for the public that differs from what the Canadian government has, to propel reconciliation for Indigenous people". I didn't necessarily state this redundantly across my essays, but did relate their importance back to this main theme in either the Introduction or Conclusion paragraph of every essay.

Chosen Media Form

The reason why I chose to code this website, rather than make it from Google Sites, is because of the experience I have with making similar ones. I simply don't know how to make a good-looking website, other than by coding it. I'm not too comfortable with other platforms such as Google Sites, having only used them for a couple projects. Whereas, I've coded about a dozen websites similar to this one. This is how I felt I could present what I had in as quick of a way I could. Also, it gave me the liberty to do certain things my way, rather than following a restricting template. Especially, since this assingment isn't too demanding in terms of how its presented technologically, I felt better about doing this in the best way I know how.
